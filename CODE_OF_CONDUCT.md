# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want Axiom-PMO to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, identity, or the tools they use.
We are committed to a respectful and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and considerate in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good faith and critique ideas, not people.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Respect the project's governance principles — especially that human authority
  and evidence requirements are features, not obstacles.
- Disclose AI-assisted contributions honestly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or the community.
- Submitting knowingly falsified evidence, approvals, or test results.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues,
pull requests, discussions — and in public spaces when an individual is
representing the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
project maintainer through the contact channel listed in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).
Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith. The maintainer is
responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate corrective action,
including removing contributions or restricting participation, in response to
behavior deemed inappropriate.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from widely used open-source community
guidelines, notably the Contributor Covenant, and rewritten for this project.
